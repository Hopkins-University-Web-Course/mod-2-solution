Chicken
Like other female birds, hens lay eggs. The eggs hatch into chicks. When raising chickens, a farmer needs to build a chicken coop (like a little house) for the chickens to roost (sleep) in.

Beef
Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle, particularly skeletal muscle. Humans have been eating beef since prehistoric times.

Sushi
Sushi is a Japanese dish of specially prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, combined with a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.
